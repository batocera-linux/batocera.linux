NVRAM files store arcade machine hardware configuration etc.
We have provided generic NVRAM files to enable access to poluar games without additional configuration in the emulator.

If you are having problems with a games starting - i.e. Network error then:

1. Ensure you have a keyboard plugged into your emulation machine.
2. Hit key 6 to bring up the service menu.
3. Hit key 5 to navigate to 'Game Assignments'.
4. Use key 6 to enter sub menu.
5. Typically changing Link ID to Single will fix the problem.
6. Exit & then Exit again to play the game.
